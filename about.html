On-Ear Headphones (Supra-aural) On-ear headphones, or Supra-aural headphones, provide a middle ground between the over-ear and earbud style. They rest comfortably on the outer ear, providing a comfortable fit without fully enclosing the ear. This design offers a balanced, natural sound quality while maintaining a relatively compact size.
